Why RBI

Protect. Develop. Prepare. Operate.

The goal is not to replace coaches. The goal is to give coaches and parents better decision support.

Protect

Prevent overuse and manage athlete workload with clearer pitcher availability guidance.

Develop

Build better players through objective feedback, mechanical checkpoints, and drill plans.

Prepare

Use scouting, athlete context, and game plans to gain an edge before first pitch.
